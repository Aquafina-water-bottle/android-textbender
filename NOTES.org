* Notes
- When updating SDK/build tools versions, the following must be changed:
  - app/build.gradle
    - ~compileSdk~
    - ~buildToolsVersion~
    - ~targetSdk~
    - ~sourceCompatibility~
    - ~targetCompatibility~
    - ~jvmTarget~
  - app/src/main/AndroidManifest.xml
    - ~targetApi~
  - flake.nix
- When releasing a new version:
  - app/build.gradle
    - ~versionCode~
    - ~versionName~
